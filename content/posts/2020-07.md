\sum_{n=0}^\infty I^n(1)=\left(\sum\limits_{n=0}^\infty\right)(1)=\frac{I^0}{I^0-I}(1)=(I^0-I)^{-1}(1) \\\

y-\int\limits^x_0y(t)dt=1 |\frac{d}{dx} \\\
y'-y=0 \Rightarrow y=e^x+C \\\
e^x+C-e^x+1-Cx=1=1+C(1-x) \Rightarrow C=0\ and\ y=e^x
